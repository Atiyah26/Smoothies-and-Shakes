SMOOTHIE RECIPES

Ingrediants

  • 11/2 cups apple juice
  • 1 banana
  • 11/2 cups frozen blueberries
  • 3/4 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Place the apple juice, blueberries +greek yogurt in a blender
  2. Blend until completely smooth
  3. Pour into glass and serve, top with blueberries and mint if desired

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 large avocado
  • 2 kiwis peeled
  • 1/8 cup lime juice
  • 1/8 cup-1/4 cup almond milk, fruit juice
  • 2-3 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Place the liquid in the blender first, then fruit and ice
  2. Start on low speed, increasing to high speed
  3. Blend it up until creamy + very smooth

Ingrediants

  • 11/2 cups apple/orange juice
  • 1 banana
  • 3/4 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • 11/2 cups frozen mango chunks
  • lime slices + mint springs for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the juice, banana, greek yogurt + mango chunks in the blender
  2. Blend until completely smooth
  3. Pour and serve

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cucumber peeled
  • 1 pear cored+chopped
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 6-7 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingrediants in the order listed
  2. Blend till smooth and creamy
  3. Pour and serve

Ingrediants

  • 11/2 cups pineapple juice

Instructions

Ingrediants

  • 1 apple
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 rolled oats
  • 1/2 unchopped frozen banana
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Core the apple
  2. Put apple, almond milk, rolled oats, banana and peanut butter in blender
  3. Pour and serve

Ingrediants

  • 14oz(400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 frozen bananas + 2 regular bananas
  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 6 dates
  • 1 cup ice

Instructions

  1. Place coconut milk, frozen and regular bananas, almonds, dates and ice in blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour and serve
