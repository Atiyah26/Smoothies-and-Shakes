KILLER MILKSHAKES

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1/2 of a store-bought cheesecake, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz. cream cheese, room temp
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 5 Tbsp. powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Add ice cream, cheesecake pieces and graham cracker crumbs to a blender
  2. Blend until completely smooth
  3. Pour milkshake into glasses and top with cream cheese whipped cream

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup salted caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream, chilled
  • 1 tbsp powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Add ice cream, whole milk and salted caramel sauce to a blender. Blend until smooth. You can add more caramel if desired.
  2. Whip the chilled cream until it reaches soft peaks. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla; beat until cream holds peaks. Set aside.
  3. Pour and serve

  • Melted Chocolate
  • 2 brownies, cut into chunks
  • 4 cups chocolate or vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream

Instructions
